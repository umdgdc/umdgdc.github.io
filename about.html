ABOUT US:

UMD GDC is the premier game development student org at the University of Maryland, College Park. As a diverse community of programmers, artists, composers, and writers, we are dedicated to the advancement of game development within the student community.

There are zero restrictions for joining. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, come and join us!
